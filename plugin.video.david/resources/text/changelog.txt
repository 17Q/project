[B]How to Add Free Accounts and API Keys[/B]

[B]Trakt Information:[/B]
- Automatically Track TV Shows and Movies You're Watching
- Sign up for free at https://trakt.tv/auth/join
- Authorize your Trakt Account - From Main Menu - Click Tools - Click Settings: David - Click Meta Accounts

[B]TMDb Information:[/B]
- Personal API keys provide quicker API responses
- Sign up for free at https://www.themoviedb.org/account/signup
- Enter your TMDb API Key - From Main Menu - Click Tools - Click Settings: David - Click Meta Accounts

[B]Fanart.tv Information:[/B]
- Personal API keys provide quicker API responses
- Sign up for free at https://fanart.tv/wp-login.php?action=register
- Enter your TMDb API Key - From Main Menu - Click Tools - Click Settings: David - Click Meta Accounts

[B]IMDb Information:[/B]
- Sign up for free at https://www.imdb.com
- Enter your IMDb UR Name - From Main Menu - Click Tools - Click Settings: David - Click Meta Accounts
